Book Review

Chankya Neeti

The study of power dynamics and human behavior finds one of its most potent historical expressions in the Chanakya Neeti, a collection of aphorisms attributed to the ancient Indian strategist Kautilya, also known as Vishnugupta. Composed during the formation of the Mauryan Empire around 321 BCE, these teachings served as the pragmatic bedrock for statecraft and personal conduct and inspire many even today.

Book Review

Zero to One - Peter Thiel

Thiel rejects incrementalism, arguing that true value is created only through vertical progress—building a monopoly by doing something entirely new rather than competing in existing markets. It is a rigorous, contrarian framework that replaces standard business clichés with a demand for proprietary technology and long-term planning.
